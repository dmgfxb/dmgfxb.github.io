Miss G's Website Evaluation Report

Evaluation of Miss G's Frustration Free Tools & Strategies

Evaluator Participants:

Roni is the first evaluator, she is middle aged woman who is a friend of my aunt's. I selected her because she is a mother of a child who is in elementary school and has ADHD. He does not have a reading disability, but at times struggles reading class becuase of the lengthy time period and in turn sometimes this impacts his skill set and behavior. I believe she would have fallen somewhere within my target audience and was an appropriate candidate to evaluate the site. Therefore, it could give me a better indication if my site would be benefit students and families that do not specifically relate to the identification of a reading disability as well. She used her Dell CORE i5, Windows 7 computer with a Google chrome browser. She was connected to her home high speed internet when completing this evaluation.

My next evaluator was Aaron, a neighbor around my age who lives in my apartment complex. He is a little younger than what I pressume most of my targeted audience would be and has a good amount of technology experience and background that maybe some of the others evaluating my site may not maintain. I felt it would be beneficial to have someone with some other experience and different point of view take a look at it as well. He used a Surface Pro i7 processor, GoogleChrome for his browser and was connected to the high speed internet with in his apartment.

Lastly, I asked a parent, who's child I had in class previously, to evaluate my site. Her name is Julie and she is a hands on parent, who has a basic understanding of her child's reading disability, but still has many questions and concerns regularly and is always searching for new ideas to test at home. I selected Julie because I wanted a prime example of someone from the targeted audience because ultimately they were the reason behind my site. I want to know first hand if an active parent who wants to know and try more, found the site, useful, appropriate, user-friendly,informative, organized etc. I am really going to take everything she says into serious consideration. Julie used an HP Intel UHD with a Windows 10 operating system using internet explorer for her browser and connecting to her at home high speed internet.
