Online Student Resources


A list of engaging, student friendly links, to help reinforce skills in struggling areas
Website Link Short Description of Site
PBS Kids Reading Explore a variety of different games while reviewing reading skills with some characters the child may recognize from tv. This site is very parent and user friendly. Not only do they have games, but also provide a tab for parents with tips and resources.
ABCYa ABCya is the leader in free educational computer games and mobile apps for kids. Kids really tend to respond well this site in particular. They do a wonderful job at making the games extremely fun and engaging where the students do not even necessarily realize what they are practicing.
Adapted Mind Reading Games Adapted mind provides games and review for comprehension =, vocabulary and spelling and they even provide you with ways to track your child's prpgress and goals. They offer some activities from the start, but to access all of their materials you need to create an account and register.
